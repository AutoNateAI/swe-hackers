üîÑ Your Design Workflow

1

Gather Requirements

Ask the client: What's the business? What style do they like? What will this be used for? Get reference images if possible.

2

Generate Options

Create 5-10 variations using AI tools. Experiment with different prompts and styles until you have strong options.

3

Refine and Edit

Use Canva or editing tools to polish your top 3 choices. Add text, adjust colors, ensure proper sizing.

4

Present Options

Show the client 2-3 refined options. Explain your design choices and ask which direction they prefer.

5

Revise and Deliver

Make requested changes, export in required formats (PNG, JPG, transparent), and deliver final files.

üí° Pro Tip: Underpromise, Overdeliver

Tell clients you'll deliver 2-3 options, but give them 4-5. Include extra sizes they didn't ask for. This small extra effort leads to repeat business and referrals.
